This site works best when it's made by all of us. If you've got a good photo of
the Ridge, or a story worth sharing, we'd love to see it.

**Photos.** Trails and parkland, the view on a clear morning, gardens in bloom,
first snow, a get-together, your street in any season. Anything that captures
what it's like to live here.

**Stories and articles.** A short write-up of a walk you love, a bit of the
neighbourhood's history, a heads-up that would help your neighbours, or a
recommendation. A few paragraphs is plenty; we're happy to help tidy it up.

## How to send them

- Email them to us at [hello@ourridge.ca](mailto:hello@ourridge.ca), or
- Use the [contact form](/contact) and we'll follow up.

A couple of quick notes: please only send photos you took (or have permission to
share), and if a photo shows people who could be recognised, make sure they're
happy to have it posted. We'll always credit you unless you'd rather we didn't.

Thanks for helping tell the story of the Ridge.
