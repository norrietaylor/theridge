Once a summer the whole neighbourhood spreads out across the park lawn for a proper potluck. Long tables of home cooking, kids running between blankets, and plenty of time to sit and talk as the light softens over the ridge.

There is no theme and no fuss, bring whatever you like to make, whether that is a family favourite, a salad from the garden, or a store-bought treat. An RSVP helps us guess numbers for tables and shade, but drop-ins are always welcome too.
