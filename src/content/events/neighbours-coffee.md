New to the ridge, or simply never had the chance to meet the people a few doors down? This easy morning coffee is for you. We set up near the playground so kids can play while the grown-ups chat, and there is no agenda beyond getting to know one another.

It is a warm, low-pressure way to feel more at home in the neighbourhood. Stay for five minutes or the whole morning — either way, you will leave knowing a few more friendly faces.
